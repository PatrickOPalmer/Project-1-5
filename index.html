Introduction

Allow us to introduce Patrick Palmer, a down-to-earth musician and a valued member of our ensemble in the production of "Sweeney Todd." This marks Patrick's first step into the world of musical theatre, but his musical journey has been a heartfelt one. He's had the pleasure of contributing his talents to some unique musical acts, including "Gallows Humor," known for their quirky and darkly comedic music, and "Cutthroat Reef," a rollicking sea-shanty, pirate band.

Things Patrick Likes to do in his spare time:

  1. Perform at Ren Faires
  2. Study for the musical, Sweeney Todd
  3. Do homework

One of his favorite websites, still going strong since 1997, is Slashdot. Well, at least it's still going.
